Glossary

Please see below a list of terms used within the motor finance industry.


Term Description
Hire Purchase
(HP)		 A type of finance where the customer pays a deposit, followed by regular monthly payments over a set term, covering the full cost of the vehicle. The customer then owns the vehicle at the end of the agreement.
Personal Contract Purchase
(PCP)		 A type of finance where the customer pays a deposit, followed by reduced regular monthly payments. At the end of the agreement, the customer has the choice to either hand the car back to the dealer, or pay the residual value of the vehicle as the final payment.
Residual Value
(RV)		 The RV is the estimated cost of the vehicle at the end of the agreement term. For example, a vehicle worth £10,000 now may be estimated to be worth £4,000 after 3 years. The RV is used for the lender to be able to offer reduced monthly payments.
