Finance Calculator Plugin

Introduction

The Finance Calculator Plugin is a simple plugin used to easily add a calculator with functionality for customers to customise their quote. The plugin also has built-in videos and calls to action to assist the customer in making a decision and to continue the finance journey. The plugin also provides a function to pull out any of the details from the quote to display them elsewhere on the webpage.
